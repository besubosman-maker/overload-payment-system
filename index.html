System Overview

The Woldiya University Overload Payment Management System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline the entire process of managing teaching overloads across the university.

This system replaces manual processes with automated workflows, ensuring accuracy, transparency, and efficiency in tracking, approving, and processing overload payments for teaching staff.

Key Benefits:

• Automated workflow from request to payment
• Real-time tracking of request status
• Secure role-based access control
• Comprehensive reporting and analytics
• Reduced administrative workload
• Improved transparency and accountability

System Features

Teacher Portal

Submit overload requests, track approval status, view payment history, and manage teaching schedules

Department Head

Review, approve, or reject overload requests, assign courses, and monitor department workload

Finance Officer

Process payments, generate financial reports, manage payment rates, and ensure compliance

Administrator

Manage user accounts, configure system settings, monitor system usage, and generate analytics

How It Works

1. Submit Request

Teachers submit overload teaching requests through their portal with course details and hours

2. Department Approval

Department heads review and approve requests based on department needs and budget

3. Finance Processing

Finance officers process approved payments based on established payment rates

4. Payment & Reporting

Payments are processed and comprehensive reports are generated for all stakeholders
